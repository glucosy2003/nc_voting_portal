What is NC Voting Portal?

The NC Voting Portal (Nurses' Challenge) is a secure, transparent, and student-centered online voting system developed specifically for Daeyang University students council elections. It enables real-time elections with accountability and convenience.

Why “Nurses’ Challenge”?

The name represents a bold step: nurses challenging the norm, embracing technology, and showing leadership beyond the hospital. It encourages every nurse to explore innovation and think outside the box.

Built by a Student Nurse

Created by a passionate student nurse with no formal ICT background, this project was built using self-learning through YouTube, ChatGPT, online documentation, and support from ICT students. It proves that dedication and curiosity are more powerful than limitations.

Transparency, Security & Simplicity

  • 🔒 Secure: Only verified students can vote once.
  • 👁️ Transparent: Real-time visible results and vote counts.
  • 📱 Simple: Friendly, mobile-first design for everyone.

Vision & Impact

This system aims to inspire collaboration between Nursing and ICT students and encourage personal growth through innovation. It stands as a student-led revolution in using digital tools to improve institutional activities.

To All Students...

You don’t need to be a formal professional to innovate. You need willingness to learn. Let NC Voting Portal inspire you to discover your potential. Nurses, ICT, BBA students, and anyone at Daeyang — this is your call to CHALLENGE TECHNOLOGY.

“If a nurse can code, you can too.”
